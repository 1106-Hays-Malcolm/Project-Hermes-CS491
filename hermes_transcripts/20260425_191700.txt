LLM: To escape the Nautiloid, you need to reach the Helm and take control of the Nautiloid. To do this, follow these steps:

1. Find the surgery room by exiting the door next to the restoration device on the western side of the room at X:24Y:-423.
2. In the surgery room, interact with the Neural Apparatus in the middle of the room to raise the platform.
3. On the upper level, you will find a high elf named Myrnath with a removed scalp. Interact with Myrnath and free the intellect devourer, Us.
4. Us will task you to reach the Helm and take control of the Nautiloid.
5. Exit the surgery room and follow the south-western path that leads outside.
6. You will encounter the githyanki soldier Lae'zel, who will join your party to escape.
7. Lae'zel will insist on taking control of the Nautiloid at the Helm. To reach the Helm, you need to defeat three lesser imps.
8. There is a health restoring device at the northern side of the room at X:-39Y:-376.
9. To reach the next room, continue north by climbing the arterial meshes.
10. In the next room, you will find two unconscious victims of the mind flayers. Interact with the control panel adjacent to them to defeat the cultists.
11. Toward the north-east side of the room, you will find Shadowheart trapped in a Mind Flayer pod. Interact with Shadowheart to initiate the quest Rescue the Illithid's Captive.
12. If you want to retrieve the Everburn Blade in the next room without defeating its wielder, it is advisable for at least one party member to prepare Command.
13. When you enter the Helm, your goal is to reach the transponder on the far west side of the room. A pair of lesser imps and a lesser hellsboar block the path.
