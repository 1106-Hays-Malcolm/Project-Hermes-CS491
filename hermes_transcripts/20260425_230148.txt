User: What do I do?
LLM: 
1. First, explore the Nursery and interact with the various objects and characters to gather information and loot.
2. Next, follow the south-western path outside to find Lae'zel, a githyanki soldier who will join your party.
3. Continue north by climbing the arterial meshes to reach the next room.
4. In the centre of the next room, free the unconscious victims and defeat the lesser imps to progress.
5. Toward the north-east side of the room, you will find Shadowheart trapped in a Mind Flayer pod. You can free her or continue to the Helm.
6. In the Helm, defeat Commander Zhalk and his minions to reach the transponder and connect it to escape the Hells.
User: What should i interact with in the nursery?

LLM: In the nursery, you can interact with the following:
1. The mind flayer nursery in the centre of the room.
2. The body of a mind flayer south of the nursery.
3. The raised path leading to a cartilaginous chest farther south of the mind flayer.
4. The elevated area with an Onyx and Rune Slate toward the eastern side of the room.
5. The table containing an Illithid Record and a goblin corpse in the surgery room.
6. The three additional rune slates north-west of the table in the surgery room.
7. The Eldritch Tablet on the far north side of the room.
8. The small elevator in the middle of the surgery room.
9. The high elf named Myrnath on the upper level.
10. The Neural Apparatus on the upper level.
11. The githyanki soldier Lae'zel outside the nursery.
12. The control panel adjacent to the two unconscious victims of the mind flayers in the next room.
13. The Mind Flayer pod containing Shadowheart in the next room.
14. The locked Elaborate Reliquary at X:7Y:-361.
15. The console in the Helm.

User: In the nursery, Is there anything that will benefit me to interact with?
LLM: In the nursery, there are several items that can benefit you:
1. A mind flayer "nursery" (a pool filled with larvae) can be found in the centre of the room. Passing a  DC10 Investigation Check reveals that the casing is fragile. If touching the pool, it explodes and causes damage.
2. South of the nursery, the body of a mind flayer can be looted for a random gem at X:43Y:-429.
3. Farther south of the mind flayer, a raised path leads to a cartilaginous chest containing random loot at X:-43Y:-440.
4. Toward the eastern side of the room, another raised path leads to an elevated area, which curves along the northwest side of the room. An Onyx and Rune Slate can be found on a table at X:50Y:-408. Interacting with the slate confers visions of the Nautiloid travelling through the Planes.
5. North-west of the table, three additional rune slates can be found, which provide more images of the Nautiloid and an anomalous feeling.
6. On the far north side of the room, an Eldritch Tablet reveals images of Humanoid history.
