User: What do I do?

LLM: 
To escape the Nautiloid, you need to reach the Helm and connect the transponder. Here are the steps to do so:

1. Explore the Nursery and find the Onyx and Rune Slate.
2. Head to the surgery room and free the intellect devourer Us.
3. Follow the south-western path outside and meet Lae'zel.
4. Climb the arterial meshes to reach the next room.
5. Defeat the two unconscious victims and the cultists in the centre of the room.
6. Rescue Shadowheart if you wish, or continue to the Helm.
7. In the Helm, defeat Commander Zhalk and his minions to reach the transponder.
8. Connect the transponder to escape the Hells.
