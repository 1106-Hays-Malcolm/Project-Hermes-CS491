LLM: To escape the Nautiloid, you need to reach the Helm and take control of the Nautiloid. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Find the surgery room by entering the door next to the restoration device on the western side of the room at X:24Y:-423.
2. Interact with the Neural Apparatus in the middle of the room to raise the platform.
3. On the upper level, you'll find a high elf named Myrnath with a removed scalp. Free the intellect devourer named Us by passing a DC10 Arcana Check.
4. After freeing Us, you can choose to mutilate the creature by passing a DC15 Dexterity Check.
5. Continue north by climbing the arterial meshes to reach the next room.
6. In the centre of the next room, you'll find two unconscious victims of the mind flayers. Interact with the control panel adjacent to them and select "Aggression" to defeat the cultists and gain 10 XP.
7. Toward the north-east side of the room, you'll find Shadowheart trapped in a Mind Flayer pod. If you want to free her, you'll need to obtain an Eldritch Rune from a corpse on the far east side at X:62Y:-355.
8. Head to the Helm by following the south-western path that leads outside. You'll encounter the githyanki soldier Lae'zel, who will join your party to escape.
9. Defeat three lesser imps to reach the Helm.
10. In the Helm, defeat Commander Zhalk and his infernal allies (imps and hellsboars) to reach the transponder on the far west side of the room.
11. Connect the transponder to escape the Hells.
