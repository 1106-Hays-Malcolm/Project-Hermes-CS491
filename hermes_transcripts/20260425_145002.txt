User: what do i do?

LLM: 
To escape the Nautiloid, you need to reach the Helm and take control of the Nautiloid. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

1. Start by exploring the Nursery. Investigate the mind flayer nursery to learn more about it. You can find a Restoration device on the western side of the room to restore your health.
2. South of the nursery, you'll find a dead mind flayer. Loot it for a random gem.
3. Continue south to find a raised path leading to a cartilaginous chest containing random loot.
4. Head towards the eastern side of the room to find an elevated area with three rune slates. Interact with the slates to learn more about the Nautiloid.
5. In the middle of the room, you'll find a small elevator. Interact with the Neural Apparatus to raise the platform.
6. On the upper level, you'll find a high elf named Myrnath. Free the intellect devourer inside his exposed brain. You can ask it for its name, which is Us. Us will task you with reaching the Helm and taking control of the Nautiloid.
7. Before freeing Us, you can choose to perform a cerebral extraction. Pass a DC10 Investigation Check to perform the extraction with a DC10 Medicine Check with Advantage, or pass a DC10 Strength or Dexterity Check.
8. After freeing Us, you can choose to mutilate the creature. Pass a DC15 Dexterity Check to do so.
9. Follow the south-western path outside to find Lae'zel, a githyanki soldier who will join your party to escape.
10. Defeat three lesser imps to reach the Helm.
11. Inside the Helm, you'll find a console. Interact with it to connect the nerves of the transponder and escape the Hells.
12. Defeat Commander Zhalk and his infernal allies to reach the console. You can choose to ignore Zhalk to reach the console, or defeat him to gain an XP boost and obtain the Everburn Blade.
