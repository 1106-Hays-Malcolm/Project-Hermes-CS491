User: What do I do?
LLM: 
1. First, explore the Nursery and interact with the various objects and characters to gather information and loot.
2. Next, follow the south-western path outside to find Lae'zel, a githyanki soldier who will join your party.
3. Continue north by climbing the arterial meshes to reach the next room.
4. In the centre of the next room, free the unconscious victims and defeat the lesser imps to progress.
5. Toward the north-east side of the room, you will find Shadowheart trapped in a Mind Flayer pod. Free her if possible.
6. Head to the Helm and defeat Commander Zhalk and his minions to reach the transponder.
7. Connect the transponder to complete the quest "Escape the Nautiloid".
