User: what do I do?

LLM: 
To escape the Nautiloid, you need to reach the Helm and connect the transponder. Here are the steps to do so:

1. Explore the Nursery and find the Onyx and Rune Slate.
2. Head to the surgery room and free the intellect devourer Us.
3. Follow the south-western path outside and meet Lae'zel.
4. Climb the arterial meshes to reach the next room.
5. Defeat the two unconscious victims and the cultists in the centre of the room.
6. If Shadowheart is trapped in a Mind Flayer pod, free her using a DC7 Arcana Check (Warlocks, Wizards, and Sorcerers) or a DC10 Strength Check (Barbarians).
7. Head east to the other Ceremorphosis Chamber to obtain an Eldritch Rune if necessary.
8. Enter the Helm and defeat Commander Zhalk and his minions.
9. Reach the transponder on the far west side of the room.
10. Connect the transponder to escape the Hells.
